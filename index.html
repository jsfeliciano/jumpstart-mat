The Jumpstart into MAT® Explained: Muscle Activation Techniques 101

MAT® is designed to revitalize the communication pathways in the body and restore muscle contractile capabilities. The main indicator of muscles losing their contractile efficiency is compromised range of motion (ROM). Through the MAT® assessment, we begin with a muscle-specific joint assessment exam. This allows the MAT® practitioner to precisely determine the muscles that have lost communication with the nervous system. MAT® practitioners is trained to employ two processes designed to improve contractile capabilities. First, muscle-specific palpitation stimulates sensory receptors and then, position-specific isometrics improve motor output to the muscles.
